★ Blondies ★

Have a sweet tooth? This is the perfect recipe to wow your taste buds! Blondies are similar to Brownies, excpet we replace the cocoa powder with vanilla and contains brown sugar. I would compare it to a milk chocolate brownie.



Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 35 minutes

Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients Needed

  • 1⁄2 Cup of Unsalted Butter Melted
  • 1 Cups Packed Brown Sugar
  • 1 Egg + 1 Egg Yolk (At Room Temperature)
  • 1 Tablespoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1 Cup of All Purpose Flour
  • 1⁄4 Teaspoon of Baking Powder
  • 1⁄2 Teaspoon of Salt
  • 1 Cup of Semi Sweet Chocolate Chips

Cooking Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 ℉
  2. Line a 8x8 pan with parchment paper and set aside
  3. In a large bowl mix the melted butter and brown sugar
  4. Add the egg, egg yolk and vanilla extract and mix together
  5. In a separate bowl add the flour, baking powder, and salt and stir until well incorporated
  6. Transfer the wet ingridients into the dry ingredients and mix together
  7. Pour in chocolate chips and mix
  8. Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth out
  9. Place in the oven to bake for 25 to 30 minutes until edges are golden and a toothpick comes out mostly clean
  10. Allow to cool completely, then cut into squares
